About Me

The art of forecasting future outcomes based on historical data and trends is something that has always intrigued me. On a more mundane level, as an ardent cricket fan, I was always fascinated by how experts can scientifically forecast the outcome of the match just by analyzing the playing conditions. However, it wasn't until I was in my undergrad that I realized how pervasive the field of Data Science is and how it has permeated every aspect of our lives. It wouldn't be wrong to infer that the modern era is ruled by data, with each activity of our day-to-day life mandated by it. From our shopping preferences to educational pursuits, this potent entity is guiding most of our activities. With tons of such insightful data generated daily, professionals must start devising extremely targeted techniques to analyze and draw insights from such data.And it was this that truly caught my attention and motivated me to pursue a career in data science and artificial intelligence in particular. The saying that "With ordinary talent and extraordinary perseverance, all things are attainable." was ingrained in me right from childhood. My past education and work experience have broadened my knowledge base and provided me with a thorough understanding of various core subjects entailed in Data science.
